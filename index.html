About Me

I am a Data Science professional currently studying at the Center for Data Science, New York University. I am expected to graduate in May 2026, specializing in data science with a keen interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision (CV), and Machine-Human Interactions.

Throughout my professional journey, I have had the privilege of working in diverse domains, ranging from financial giants like Standard Chartered to cutting-edge generative AI-based startups such as Flixstock. In 2020, I interned at Standard Chartered in a data science role and became proficient in tools like Tableau and Power BI. I was also recognized with the “Best Newcomer Project” award in my first year for delivering impactful solutions. With over 2 years of professional experience across multiple roles—including web development, full-stack development, CI/CD, and product management—I have successfully contributed to creating scalable and optimized systems, real-time data pipelines, ML models, and APIs.

My educational journey began at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) BHU, Varanasi, where I earned my Bachelor of Technology degree after securing an All India Rank (AIR) of 5407 among 1.5 million students in the national entrance examination. Currently, I am pursuing my Master’s at New York University, majoring in Data Science. I am taking courses in Linear Algebra and Optimization, Probability and Statistics, and Big Data, with research interests in CV, NLP, and Machine-Human Interactions.

I look forward to giving back to the community in any way possible. During my undergraduate years, I conducted coding workshops as a member of the Club of Programmers and organized music workshops, inviting external guests as the secretary of the Music Club at IIT BHU. In my professional roles, I have also led workshops explaining engineering and data science concepts to business and marketing teams. I am always eager to mentor and learn from others.

Besides my enthusiasm for data science, I enjoy creating and listening to music and playing badminton. I am always excited to meet new people and exchange ideas. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, and let’s arrange a coffee chat!
