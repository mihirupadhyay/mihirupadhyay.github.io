About Me

I am a Data Science professional currently pursuing a Master's degree at the Center for Data Science, New York University, with an expected graduation date of May 2026. My academic focus is on Data Science, with a keen interest in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Computer Vision (CV), and Machine-Human Interactions.

My professional journey has been diverse, spanning from established financial institutions like Standard Chartered Bank to generative AI-based startups such as Flixstock. During my 2020 internship at Standard Chartered, I honed my expertise in data visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, and was honored with the “Best Newcomer Project” award for delivering impactful solutions. With 2+ years of professional experience in roles including Machine Learning, full-stack development, and product management, I have contributed to building scalable systems, real-time data pipelines, ML models, and APIs.

My educational background is rooted in the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) BHU, Varanasi, where I earned my Bachelor of Technology degree. I secured an All India Rank (AIR) of 5407 out of 1.5 million students in the JEE Advanced Exam, showcasing my academic aptitude. At NYU, I am currently engaged in courses such as Linear Algebra and Optimization, Probability and Statistics, and Big Data, while pursuing research interests in CV, NLP, and Machine-Human Interactions.

In addition to my academic and professional endeavors, I am passionate about giving back to the community. As an undergraduate, I conducted coding workshops as part of the Club of Programmers and organized music workshops as the Secretary of the Music Club at IIT BHU. In my professional roles, I have led workshops to bridge the gap between engineering and business teams by explaining complex data science and technical concepts. I am always enthusiastic about mentoring and learning from others.

Beyond data science, I have a love for music and enjoy both creating and listening to it. I also have a passion for badminton and am always excited to connect with new people and exchange ideas. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, and let's set up a coffee chat!
